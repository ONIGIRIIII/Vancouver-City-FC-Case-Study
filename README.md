## üéØ Business Challenge

Vancouver City FC, a professional football club in the BOLT Soccer League (BSL), faces significant financial pressures despite its loyal fanbase and strong community presence. The club's revenue has declined from approximately $60 million in prior years to materially lower levels, driven by:

- **Declining Stadium Attendance**: Underutilized 54,500-seat stadium with falling matchday revenue
- **Merchandise Sales Challenges**: 30% decline in online sales with poor team store performance
- **Operational Inefficiencies**: 8-day average delivery times and supply chain bottlenecks
- **Limited Fan Engagement**: Low season pass adoption and weakened international supporter base

## üìà Key Findings from Data Analysis

### Stadium Operations Analysis
- **Concert Revenue Opportunity**: Currently utilizing stadium for concerts only 7/12 months
- **Potential Additional Revenue**: $2.03M annually from expanded concert hosting
- **Current Average Concert Revenue**: $406.25K per event

### Merchandise Performance
- **Top Performers**: Jerseys generate highest revenue despite premium pricing
- **Underperformers**: Mugs, scarves, and posters show low sales volume
- **Channel Imbalance**: Heavy reliance on online channel (Team Store underperforming)
- **Customer Demographics**: 90% domestic, 10% international customer base

### Fanbase Engagement Metrics
- **Season Pass Holders**: Only 6.73% (4,750 out of 65,250 total members)
- **High-Engagement Opportunity**: 32,530 fans attending 5+ games without season passes
- **Age Demographics**: Strongest engagement in 18-25 age group (average 5.71 games attended)

## üí° Proposed Solutions

### 1. Premium Product Expansion & Merchandise Optimization
**Strategy**: Launch premium products and bundle low-performers
- **Implementation**: 
  - Custom signed jerseys and limited edition drops
  - Bundle mugs, scarves, and posters as value packages
  - Quarterly limited releases to maintain exclusivity
- **Projected Impact**: Increased average order value by 12%

### 2. Stadium Utilization Through Concerts
**Strategy**: Expand concert hosting to all 12 months
- **Implementation**:
  - Schedule concerts during non-match weeks
  - Use industry-standard turf protection
  - Target 5 additional months of concert revenue
- **Projected Impact**: +$2.03M annual revenue

### 3. Delivery Time Optimization
**Strategy**: Reduce delivery times from 8 days to under 5 days
- **Implementation**:
  - Offer express shipping options
  - Focus on domestic market first (90% of orders)
  - Improve warehouse operations in Burnaby facility
- **Projected Impact**: +10-15% repeat order rate

### 4. Season Pass Conversion & Retention
**Strategy**: Convert high-engagement fans to season pass holders
- **Implementation**:
  - Target 32,530 fans attending 5+ games
  - Launch 5-game and 10-game flex passes
  - Special pricing for students and young adults
- **Projected Impact**: +3,000 new season pass members
